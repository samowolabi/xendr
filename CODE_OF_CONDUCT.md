# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Xendr to be a respectful, useful, and welcoming project for contributors and users.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on code, design, documentation, and project outcomes.
- Assume good intent, but be direct when something needs to improve.
- Keep discussions on topic.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block contributors who violate this code of conduct.
